ABOUT ME

Hi, I'm Luca, a developer specialized in OOP, Web, Ruby, JavaScript, BDD/TDD, Unix.
I held many positions: soccer player, astronaut, sheriff, criminal, doctor, jet pilot, troublemaker. All before the age of 10. Now I spend my time hanging around, teaching machines how to do their dirty jobs, photographing and collecting music.
